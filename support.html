Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the SourcePoint app from the App Store, open it, and tap "Sign Up" on the login screen. Enter your email, create a username, and set a password. You'll be ready to start exploring!

How does the fact-checking feature work?

Our AI-powered fact-checking uses Google Vertex AI to verify claims in real-time. Simply navigate to the Fact Check tab, enter a statement you want to verify, and our AI will analyze it against reliable sources and provide a credibility rating with citations.

What are Dossiers?

Dossiers are personal research collections where you can save episodes, sources, and fact-checked information on topics that interest you. Create a dossier from the Dossiers tab, give it a name, and start adding content!

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days. If you need help, contact us at privacy@sourcepoint.app.

Is SourcePoint free to use?

Yes! SourcePoint is completely free to use. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will always be free.

Which platforms do you support?

SourcePoint currently supports iOS (iPhone and iPad). Android support is planned for the future. We also aggregate content from Rumble, YouTube, and other podcast platforms.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you see content that violates our terms of service, please email us at support@sourcepoint.app with details about the content and where you found it. We take all reports seriously.

Can I export my research?

Yes! You can export your dossiers and saved content. This feature is accessible from your profile settings. You have full ownership of your data.
